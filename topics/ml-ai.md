# Machine Learning & AI

I apply machine learning techniques to accelerate computational chemistry methods, improve uncertainty quantification, and enable new approaches to molecular discovery.

## Research Areas

### Method Acceleration
- Neural network acceleration of SCF convergence
- Machine learning models for integral evaluation
- Hybrid quantum-classical workflows for efficiency

### Uncertainty Quantification
- Ensemble methods for confidence estimation
- Bayesian approaches for predictive uncertainty
- Robust training for noisy experimental data

### Molecular Discovery
- Generative models for novel molecular design
- Active learning for efficient exploration of chemical space
- Graph neural networks for property prediction

## Applications

My ML work intersects with quantum chemistry through:
- Accelerating traditional electronic structure calculations
- Designing molecules with target properties
- Integrating multi-fidelity data sources
- Building interpretable models for chemical insight

## Technical Approach

I emphasize:
- Integration with established quantum chemistry packages
- Uncertainty-aware predictions for reliable results
- Interpretability and explainability in model design
- Transfer learning and domain adaptation

## Future Directions

Current work focuses on:
- ML-accelerated fragment-based methods
- Uncertainty quantification for large-scale systems
- Generative models for catalyst discovery
- Multi-scale ML-QM hybrid approaches
